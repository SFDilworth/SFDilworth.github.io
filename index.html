About Me

I'm a University of Maryland student studying journalism with a minor in creative writing. Currently, I'm the Editor in Chief of The Writer's Bloc which is an arts, music and culture campus publication based around the College Park and D.C. area. Outside of editing, I write freelance articles for other campus publications, work with the poetry board for Stylus Literary Magazine and make blog posts about music artists I enjoy.

Outside of the work I've done in several news writing classes, I've focused mostly on campus entertainment in my published pieces. I've had a passion for music and all things involving the arts since I was younger so I've naturally taken an interest in covering events like comedy shows or live performances. With the ongoing pandemic, in-person events have been very limited so I've had to get a bit creative when finding something to write about, but my hope is that I'm able to put a smile on the faces of those who read it. In the future, I hope to continue coverage of live events as everything transitions back to normal and I continue my career as a journalist.
